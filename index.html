Gabatarwar Intizarul Imamul Muntazar

Bismihi Ta'ala.

Tarbiyya wani abu ne mai muhimmancin gaske wanda baya bukatar kafa hujja ko bayani kan muhimmancin sa kuma babban aiki ne da ya kunshi bangarori uku; Gida, Makaranta da Al'umma (muhalli), bai takaita akan Iyaye kadai ba kawai.

INTIZARUL IMAMUL MUNTAZAR motsi ne da aka samar don yara yan kasa da shekaru 15, karkashin Mu'assasatu Abul Fadlil Abbas (A.S) na harkar musulunci karkashin jagorancin Sheikh Ibraheem Zakzaky (H).

An shirya intizar ne domin tattalin yara da tarbiyyantar da ruhin su da tunanin su domin share fagen ga baban aikin tarbar Sahibul Asr Waz-Zaman (AJF), wanda wannan ne mafificin ibadah a wannan zamanin.

Intizar na matsayin gudunmawa ga iyaye ga iyaye da makarantu don inganta tarbiyyan yaran su, don haka ne intizar ta tanadi tsare-tsare da ayyuka da suka ginu a kan ilimi, kyawawan dabi'u, wayewar Musulunci da hidima ga Al'umma.

Ta hanyar wadannan tsare-tsaren ne da intizar ke gudanarwa (mako-mako) a ke fatan yaran da ke halarta su zama shiryayyu don taimakon Sahibul Asr Waz-Zaman (AJF) gabadaya.

Intizar abu ne na ganin dama da sadaukarwa, don haka shiga intizar, don haka shiga intizar abune mai sauki haka ma fita, ana shiga ne ta hanyar halartar ayyukan mako-mako sau biyar a jere, kuma ana fita ne ta hanyar daina halartar ayyukan sau uku a jere.

Manufofi da ∆òa'idodi na Intizar:

  • Manufa (Vision): Samar ma Imam Mahdi (AJF) mataimaka
  • Manhaja (Mission): Bai wa yara tarbiyyar ruhi, jiki da tunani don kasancewa na gari kuma shiryayyu don taimakon Sahibul Asr Waz-Zaman
  • ∆òa'idodi (Values): AMANA, KHIDMATUN-NAS, GIRMAMAWA, GASKIYA, IKHLASI, KAMUN KAI, ILIMI, TAKAWA, TAUSAYAWA, SADAUKARWA, AIKI, BIYAYYA GA IYAYE, INTIZAR & WILAYA

"Allah ya bamu dacewa, Allahumma Ajjil-li-waliyyikal-Faraj."
